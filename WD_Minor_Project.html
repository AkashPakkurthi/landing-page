Welcome to MANGO mobiles

Mango, is an Indian multinational electronics company, that specializes in smartphones founded in March 2000, headquarters in Chennai, India. It was established as an IT software company operating in the embedded devices domain. It later entered the mobile handset business. Mango Informatics Limited is one of the leading consumer electronics company in India and the 10th largest mobile phone player in the world.
